Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

HomeIQ for Movers ("we", "us") provides moving-survey and quoting software to moving companies. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account & company data: your name, email, company name, branding (logo, color), and team roles.
  • Job data you enter: customer details, addresses, move dates, and the inventory you capture for each job.
  • Photos you capture: room and item photos used to generate inventories. These are processed by our AI provider to identify items and are not used to train third-party models.
  • Usage & billing: basic app usage and, if you subscribe, subscription status. Payment card details are handled by our payment processor (Stripe) and never stored by us.

How we use it

  • To provide the service — building inventories, generating branded quotes, and syncing your team.
  • To process subscriptions and support requests.
  • To improve reliability and performance of the app.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share it only with the service providers that run the product: our cloud/database provider, our AI provider (for item recognition), and our payment processor (Stripe, for billing). Each processes data only to provide their part of the service.

Data retention & deletion

Your company controls its own data. You can delete your account from within the app, which removes your associated records. Contact us to request deletion of company data.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit and access is restricted to your company via row-level security. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email sales@homeiqmovers.com.
